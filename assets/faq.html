Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Adding Packages

Packages are added by simply visiting their GoDep site.
Example: For github.com/golang/dep go to godep.org/github.com/golang/dep

Why do you only support GitHub?

For now we focused on GitHub, simply because it's the most popular development platform and used by most Go projects.
We plan on supporting at least as many platforms as godoc.org does

I am missing feature X

In case you're missing a feature please use the GitHub issues to open a new one, so we can discuss there.
